About Me

A bit about me and this blog

Hi, I’m Melisande! I started knitting a few months ago and it quickly became one of my favourite way to relax and make things I actually wear while expressing my creativity.

This site is where I keep my finished projects and a wishlist of patterns I’d like to try next. I love trying new techniques and always learning to improve my skills while making things I love !

I hope you like it ! Thank you for stopping by !
